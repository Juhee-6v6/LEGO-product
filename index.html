  • Whether you’re reliving past adventures, planning future travels or simply want to celebrate your passion for camper vans, you’ll love this LEGO® Volkswagen T2 Camper Van (10279) model building project. Take your time building the camper van model then explore its detailed interior before placing it on display to remind you that there’s a world of adventure just around the corner.

    Packed with authentic details
    Camper van fans will love all the neat design details packed into this VW model. New LEGO elements recreate much-loved features from the real camper van, including the iconic front windscreen, textile curtains and the textile pop-up tent. There’s also a new LEGO tire shape, working steering, a sliding door, surfboard, 2 folding chairs and flower-power stickers.

    Experience the journey
    This set is part of a collection of LEGO building sets for adults who appreciate clever design and attention-to-detail. It offers an immersive build and will make a top gift for any VW camper fan.

    Fabulous Nostalgia!

    I would recommend this to a friend!

    I am a big fan of the Creator Export line and when I saw this released I ordered right away. The color is awesome and I was excited to get towards the end. Upon completion the roof has a gap up front and could not figure what the issue was. I reviewed the instructions in detail and even rebuilt bag #10 and #11 and for the life of me, can get the roof to sit flush. So my excitement to finish resulted in a bit of frustration and my OCD is getting the better of me. I even took off the canopy cloth to see if that was causing it and still has a gap Only option is to display with roof open.
